Let's Talk!

I am a fourth year design student at UC Davis, interested in making unique connections between UI/UX and graphic design, architecture, and the natural world. I hope to collaborate on products that are sustainable and emphasize broad user accessibility. I am passionate about honing a diversified skillset that brings me closer to my goal of becoming a "UI/UX Unicorn".

When I'm not making art or finding a cat to pet, I'm probably cultivating my photography skills! A designer is nothing without process documentation, and I aim to include more curated images and real-life mockups to give future projects a personalized touch.
