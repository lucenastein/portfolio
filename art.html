Selected Works

Presented is a non-chronological gallery of my artistic work, spanning more traditional mediums (such as collage, graphite, and charcoal) and digital art (made primarily using Procreate and Adobe Photoshop). Having taken many art classes over the course of my academic career, I like to bring my knowledge of color theory, composition, and shape language into my visual design work.
