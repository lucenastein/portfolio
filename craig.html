Craigslist Redesign

December 2024
Design Interactive Club Fellowship
As part of Design Interactive's Fall Fellowship, I worked in a team of designers on a six-week sprint to redesign the Craigslist website. The pain-points we identified mostly centered around a cluttered and confusing UI, with extensive buried information and poor organization. For our final design, we aimed to both intuitively guide users through a clean site structure, and present a polished, customer safety and satisfaction oriented brand. My process included creating a new brand kit and Figma prototype with these guidelines in mind. I was also responsible for creating a brand-oriented hero image for the home page, and concise navigation which took into account the most visited parts of Craigslist.
