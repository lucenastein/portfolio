Caecilia Poster

October 2024
Course: Graphic Design & Computer Technology
For this assignment, I was tasked with creating a poster that holistically represented the font family PMN Caecilia. My constraints included sizing and information: I had to showcase Caecilia's lowercase and uppercase letterforms, numbers, and history. My process involved doing extensive research on Caecilia and its creator, Peter Matthias Noordzij. I used soft colors and white, window-like shapes, playing with transparency to both emphasize Caecilia's curving, humanistic forms and evoke early 2000s-2010s web browsers (specifically the Kindle screens where Caecilia would become the default). I also created two alternate designs (shown below) but felt they were less conceptually strong than my final--though still an important part of my design process.
